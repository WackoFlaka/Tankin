Just a funny guy

Welcome to Tankin’s world! Tankin is the guy who can make anyone laugh, usually by kicking off conversations with a bold “Hey, bitch!” He’s known for his hilarious one-liners, playful comebacks, and an uncanny ability to bring a smile to everyone’s face. If there’s one thing Tankin does best, it’s keeping things light and entertaining.

Tankin isn’t just another funny guy; he’s the funny guy. With his unique humor, Tankin knows how to keep things real. His signature style—always a little brash, always a lot of fun—makes him a favorite among friends and gamers alike.

DESBONES

When he heads over to his buddy Desbone’s place, it’s an experience all its own. Tankin can’t help but crack a joke about the smoky haze that fills the room—Desbone’s signature cigarette smell hanging in the air. “Smells like you lit up the whole pack in here!” he’ll say with a grin, half-joking, half-used to it by now. But Tankin has his reasons for making these visits. He’s got a soft spot for Desbone’s two cats, who always seem to be lounging around, ready for a scratch behind the ears. Tankin may be a gamer and a jokester, but he’s also an animal lover at heart, and those cats always brighten his day.
